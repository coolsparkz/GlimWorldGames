🔐 Security Policy
We take the security of GlimWorldGames seriously and aim to respond quickly to any reports of vulnerabilities. If you believe you’ve found a security issue, please follow the guidelines below to ensure responsible disclosure.

📣 Reporting a Vulnerability
If you discover a vulnerability, please report it privately so we can investigate and resolve the issue without exposing users to risk.
Your repository already lists the preferred reporting method in the Security section .

Contact Methods:

Email: lomov0@eq.edu.au

Do Not Publicly Disclose: Please avoid sharing vulnerabilities publicly until they have been fixed .

🛡️ Our Commitment
We will acknowledge your report as soon as possible.

We will provide updates as we investigate the issue.

We will notify you when the vulnerability has been resolved.

We will credit responsible disclosures when appropriate.

Thank you!
